SEARCH CONFIGURATION (shared by all runs)
  Outer loop : OpenEvolve (AlphaEvolve-style), 3 islands, 200 iterations/island,
               qwen3.6 mutator, seeded initial program = the smooth sqrt-t SEI model
               (openevolve/initial_program.py).
  Inner loop : DMCI exact-gradient Adam, adam_iters=60 during SEARCH (fast fitness).
  Re-score   : rigorous adam_iters=300 at BOTH held-out splits
               (KSPLIT_LATE=70, KSPLIT_EARLY=45) -- exposes any search-time
               fitness-fidelity flattery (the FluZoo lesson).
